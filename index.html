Frequently Asked Questions

What is Web3?

+

Web3 is the new generation of the internet built on decentralized blockchain technology. It emphasizes user ownership, digital assets, and peer-to-peer interactions without traditional intermediaries.

How can Web3 benefit my business?

+

Transitioning to Web3 can provide your business with enhanced user trust through transparency, security, and decentralized frameworks.

How does your blockchain development process work?

+

Our blockchain development process consists of four main phases: Needs analysis and planning, technical design and architectural decisions, development and testing process, and finally deployment and maintenance. We maintain close communication with our clients at every stage to ensure the project progresses according to its objectives. We place special emphasis on security and scalability.

What services do you offer for NFT projects?

+

We offer end-to-end solutions for your NFT projects. These services include smart contract development, metadata management, IPFS integration, minting website creation, marketplace integration, and technical consulting. We also help you plan secondary market strategies for your NFTs and set up royalty systems. We provide integration with popular NFT marketplaces like OpenSea and Rarible.

What does smart contract security audit cover?

+

Our smart contract security audit is a comprehensive process. We perform manual code review, automated security scans, gas optimization, tests against known vulnerabilities, and stress testing. We specifically test your contracts against re-entrancy attacks, integer overflow/underflow, front-running, and other common security issues. We provide a detailed report and recommendations for resolving any identified issues.
